Why Enterprises Need Their Own Skill Hub

Public skill marketplaces present significant challenges for enterprise adoption.

  • Security Blind Spots — Malicious code, hidden backdoors, data exfiltration
  • Compliance Gaps — No audit trails of skill usage for regulated industries
  • Quality Control Issues — Varying skill quality with no standardized testing
  • Knowledge Leakage — Exposing internal processes to third parties

Security-First Architecture

Every skill is analyzed before it reaches your agents.

  • Pattern-Based Scanning — 67+ vulnerability patterns detected automatically
  • AI-Powered Analysis — Semantic threat detection for subtle vulnerabilities
  • Complete Audit Trails — Full compliance tracking for every action
  • Team Collaboration — Fine-grained access controls and permissions
